Grades Dashboard

Create User

Please enter your Google Account email below once you have linked your account through Google Assistant. Then, enter your PowerSchool information as you would when signing in normally. Finally, enter your base_url which is the URL that you use to access PowerSchool in your district. For example, it might look like https://ps.district.org. Using https is recommended to secure your requests.










When you click the "Save" button, your information will be saved in the Dashboard database. Then you can use Dashboard through Google Assistant.

Edit Classes

To edit your classes, make sure that you have registered above first. Then, enter your email and PowerSchool password below. Finally, edit the template JSON below to create a mapping between the class names that appear in PowerSchool, for example (H) Organic Chemistry or AP Physics: C: Mechanics. In other words, it must appear exactly how it does in PowerSchool. Finally, it is reccomended to make your edits in JSONLint and verify them so there are no errors. Make sure to add a comma if there is something after it!

These synonyms are lowercase only. You can stack these synonyms if you want them to refer to the same class, for example ["physics","science"]. In addition, each synonym has multiple ways to be said and recognized by Google Assistant. For example, the art synonym can be said as art, pottery, clay, digital art, and AP art.

Do you say something another way? Send an email to picoplanetdev@gmail.com and it will be added to the list.








When you click the "Update" button, any current classes associated with your email will be replaced with the new classes in the box above.
